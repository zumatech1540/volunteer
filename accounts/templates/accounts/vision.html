Our Vision for Laikipia

Building a prosperous, inclusive, innovative, and empowered Laikipia through transformative leadership and sustainable development.

Priority Program

🏗 KILIMO BIASHARA

The Kilimo Biashara (Agribusiness Transformation)* agenda seeks to transform Laikipia into a high-productivity agricultural powerhouse through modern, inclusive, and market-driven farming. The strategy focuses on expanding irrigation to support year-round food production, establishing horticulture processing and cold-chain facilities to reduce post-harvest losses, and modernizing agricultural extension services through farmer training, digital advisory systems, and adoption of new technologies. It also aims to strengthen meat and dairy value chains through improved livestock breeds and animal health systems, while promoting feedlots and pasture development to increase livestock productivity. To diversify incomes and create more opportunities for communities, the plan proposes community fisheries in water pans, improved market access for local and export produce, expanded agricultural financing through credit guarantee schemes and agribusiness funds, and stronger support for youth agripreneurship and mechanization programs. Overall, the initiative positions agriculture not just as subsistence farming, but as a profitable driver of jobs, investment, food security, and economic transformation for the people of Laikipia.

Development Goal

💧 ACCESS TO WATER

The Access to Water agenda is focused on guaranteeing water security for households, agriculture, and industry as a foundation for sustainable development in Laikipia. The initiative aims to provide clean and reliable domestic water to all households, improving public health, sanitation, and quality of life for communities across the county. At the same time, it prioritizes water for production by expanding access to irrigation, supporting livestock farming, and enabling manufacturing and agribusiness growth. By investing in water infrastructure and sustainable water management, the program seeks to strengthen food security, create economic opportunities, and ensure that families, farmers, and businesses have the reliable water supply needed to thrive.

Economic Growth

💰 ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT

The Enterprise & Business Development agenda is designed to catalyze job creation, manufacturing, and entrepreneurship across Laikipia by creating an enabling environment for businesses to grow and thrive. The strategy focuses on promoting manufacturing and local production to increase value addition and expand economic opportunities, while strengthening financing linkages to improve access to capital for entrepreneurs and enterprises. It also aims to support Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) through training, networking, and business-friendly policies that encourage innovation and competitiveness. By expanding innovation hubs, incubation centres, and digital enterprise programs, the initiative seeks to empower youth and entrepreneurs to participate in the modern economy. In addition, the agenda prioritizes formalizing and scaling informal businesses, while strengthening cooperatives and value-chain business models to promote collective growth, higher incomes, and sustainable economic transformation throughout the county.

Urban Transformation

🏛 SMART TOWNS PROGRAM

The Smart Towns Program is focused on transforming urban centres into modern, clean, and well-planned towns that attract talent, investment, and tourism. Through improved drainage systems, enhanced street lighting, greener public spaces, and effective solid waste management, the program seeks to create safer and more livable environments for residents and businesses. It also prioritizes the upgrading of urban infrastructure across all towns while expanding digital infrastructure and smart services to support economic growth, innovation, and efficient service delivery. By investing in sustainable urban development, the program aims to position towns as vibrant hubs of opportunity, enterprise, and inclusive progress.
